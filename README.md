## AI Impact on Jobs – 2030 Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## Project Objective
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and automation are expected to reshape jobs by 2030.

## Dataset Used
<a href =https://github.com/venkateshankisetty12/Power-BI-Dashboard/blob/main/AI_Impact_on_Jobs_2030.csv>Dataset</a>

## Questions(KPIS)
- What percentage of jobs are at high risk of automation by 2030?
- Which job roles offer the highest salaries in an AI-driven economy?
- Which skills are growing the fastest in demand?
- What is the future demand outlook for jobs across industries?
- What are the most critical skills required to stay relevant in 2030?
- Which jobs offer high salary with low automation risk?
- How does salary growth vary with experience?
- Which industries are most affected by AI automation?

## Process
- Defined the objective to analyze the impact of AI on jobs focusing on automation risk, salary trends, and future demand.
- Collected and cleaned the dataset using Power Query by handling null values, correcting data types, and renaming columns.
- Transformed the data by creating new fields like experience groups and automation categories for better analysis.
- Built KPIs using DAX to measure metrics such as high-risk jobs percentage, salary insights, and skill demand.
- Developed interactive visualizations in Power BI including bar charts, line charts, scatter plots, and KPI cards.
- Designed the dashboard with proper layout, filters, and storytelling to deliver meaningful business insights.
